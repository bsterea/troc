# Riscuri

## Scop

Acest document identifica principalele riscuri ale proiectului Troc.

---

## Riscuri tehnice

- Pierderea bazei de date
- Coruperea fisierelor
- Defectiuni hardware
- Costuri de gazduire mai mari decat estimarea initiala

---

## Riscuri privind utilizatorii

- Conturi false
- Utilizatori care nu raspund
- Produse inexistente
- Schimburi abandonate

---

## Riscuri juridice

- Publicarea de produse interzise
- Incalcarea legislatiei nationale
- Reclamatii GDPR

---

## Riscuri sociale

- Spam
- Fraude
- Hartuire
- Folosirea platformei pentru activitati comerciale mascate

---

## Riscuri financiare

- Lipsa fondurilor pentru gazduire
- Cresterea costurilor de infrastructura
- Dependenta de furnizori externi

---

## Strategia de reducere a riscurilor

- Dezvoltare etapizata
- Documentatie completa
- Backup regulat
- Transparenta
- Respectarea legislatiei
- Costuri operationale cat mai mici

# Risks

## Purpose

This document identifies the main risks associated with the Troc project.

---

## Technical Risks

- Database loss
- File corruption
- Hardware failures
- Higher hosting costs than expected

---

## User Risks

- Fake accounts
- Unresponsive users
- Non-existent products
- Abandoned exchanges

---

## Legal Risks

- Publication of prohibited items
- Violations of national laws
- GDPR-related complaints

---

## Social Risks

- Spam
- Fraud
- Harassment
- Hidden commercial activity

---

## Financial Risks

- Lack of funding
- Infrastructure cost increases
- Dependence on external providers

---

## Risk Mitigation Strategy

- Incremental development
- Complete documentation
- Regular backups
- Transparency
- Legal compliance
- Low operational costs
