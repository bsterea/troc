# Troc Vision

## Mission

Troc is a civic platform created to enable direct exchanges of goods and services between private individuals.

The purpose of Troc is to strengthen local communities, encourage self-sufficiency, and reduce dependency on large commercial intermediaries.

Troc is designed as a digital infrastructure for voluntary exchange, based on trust, reciprocity, and local cooperation.

---

## Core Principles

### Private Individuals Only

Troc is intended exclusively for private individuals.

Companies, corporations, legal entities, commercial organizations, and institutional sellers are not allowed.

---

### No Money Transactions

Troc does not facilitate payments.

The platform does not process money, bank cards, cryptocurrencies, loans, or financial products.

---

### No Digital Currency

Troc units are only a reference value used to compare exchanges.

They are not a currency, cryptocurrency, investment product, security, voucher, or payment instrument.

---

### Direct Exchange

Users exchange directly with each other.

Troc acts only as a communication and matching platform.

---

### Local Communities First

The platform encourages local production, local services, local food, and local cooperation.

---

### User Ownership

Users remain owners of their goods, services, and personal data.

Users can permanently delete their accounts and personal data at any time.

---

### Transparency

The platform shall operate under transparent rules that are publicly documented.

---

## Long-Term Vision

The long-term goal of Troc is to become a national platform that allows Romanian citizens to cooperate directly, exchange value without commercial intermediaries, and strengthen local economic resilience.

Troc is intended to support households, producers, craftsmen, service providers, and local communities.
# Viziunea Troc

## Misiune

Troc este o platforma civica destinata schimburilor directe de bunuri si servicii intre persoane fizice.

Scopul Troc este consolidarea comunitatilor locale, incurajarea autosuficientei si reducerea dependentei de intermediarii comerciali de mari dimensiuni.

Troc este conceput ca o infrastructura digitala pentru schimburi voluntare, bazate pe incredere, reciprocitate si cooperare locala.

---

## Principii fundamentale

### Doar persoane fizice

Troc este destinat exclusiv persoanelor fizice.

Companiile, corporatiile, persoanele juridice, organizatiile comerciale si vanzatorii institutionali nu sunt admisi pe platforma.

---

### Fara tranzactii in bani

Troc nu faciliteaza plati.

Platforma nu proceseaza bani, carduri bancare, criptomonede, credite sau alte produse financiare.

---

### Troc nu este moneda

Unitatile Troc reprezinta doar o valoare orientativa folosita pentru compararea schimburilor.

Ele nu reprezinta moneda, criptomoneda, instrument financiar, voucher, titlu de valoare sau mijloc de plata.

---

### Schimb direct

Utilizatorii realizeaza schimburile direct intre ei.

Troc actioneaza exclusiv ca platforma de comunicare si conectare intre utilizatori.

---

### Comunitatile locale pe primul loc

Platforma incurajeaza productia locala, serviciile locale, hrana locala si cooperarea intre membrii comunitatilor.

---

### Proprietatea utilizatorului

Utilizatorii raman proprietarii bunurilor, serviciilor si datelor personale.

Orice utilizator isi poate sterge permanent contul si datele personale asociate acestuia.

---

### Transparenta

Platforma functioneaza pe baza unor reguli clare, publice si accesibile tuturor utilizatorilor.

---

## Viziune pe termen lung

Obiectivul pe termen lung al Troc este dezvoltarea unei platforme nationale care sa permita cetatenilor Romaniei sa coopereze direct, sa faca schimb de valoare fara intermediari comerciali si sa consolideze rezilienta economica a comunitatilor locale.

Troc isi propune sa sprijine gospodariile, producatorii locali, meseriasii, furnizorii de servicii si comunitatile care doresc sa colaboreze direct.
