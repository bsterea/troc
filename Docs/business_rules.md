# Business Rules

## Purpose

This document defines the core rules that govern the Troc platform.

These rules describe how users, items, exchanges, messages, and personal data must behave inside the system.

---

## User Rules

- Each user must be a private individual.
- Legal entities, companies, NGOs, associations, public institutions, and commercial sellers are not allowed.
- One email address can be used for one account only.
- A user must accept the Terms and Privacy Policy before using the platform.
- A user must provide at least county and city in order to publish items.
- A user can edit their profile.
- A user can delete their account.
- A deleted account cannot be recovered.

---

## Account Status

A user account can have one of the following statuses:

- active
- suspended
- deleted

Rules:

- Active users can publish items and send exchange offers.
- Suspended users cannot publish items or send exchange offers.
- Deleted users cannot log in and their personal data must be removed or anonymized.

---

## Item Rules

- Each item belongs to one user.
- An item can be a good or a service.
- An item must have a title, description, category, location, and estimated Troc value.
- An item can have one or more photos.
- An item must not contain illegal, dangerous, stolen, counterfeit, or prohibited goods.
- An item can be edited by its owner only.
- An item can be deleted by its owner only.

---

## Item Status

An item can have one of the following statuses:

- available
- reserved
- exchanged
- removed

Rules:

- Available items can receive exchange offers.
- Reserved items are temporarily blocked while an accepted exchange is in progress.
- Exchanged items are no longer available.
- Removed items are hidden from public search.

---

## Troc Value Rules

- Troc value is only an estimated reference value.
- Troc value does not represent money.
- Troc value cannot be converted into lei, euro, or any other currency.
- Troc value cannot be withdrawn, transferred, sold, or purchased.
- Users are free to agree on exchanges even if estimated values are different.

---

## Exchange Rules

An exchange offer can have one of the following statuses:

- pending
- accepted
- rejected
- cancelled
- completed

Rules:

- A user can send an exchange offer for an available item.
- The receiver can accept or reject the offer.
- The sender can cancel a pending offer.
- Once accepted, the involved item may become reserved.
- Once completed, the exchange cannot be changed.
- Troc is not a party to the exchange.
- The final responsibility belongs to the users involved.

---

## Message Rules

- Messages are allowed only between users involved in an exchange interest or exchange offer.
- Messages must not be used for illegal activity, harassment, threats, spam, or commercial advertising.
- Users are responsible for the content they send.
- Messages may be deleted or anonymized when an account is deleted, depending on legal and technical requirements.

---

## GDPR and Data Rules

- Users own their personal data.
- Users can request access to their personal data.
- Users can edit their personal data.
- Users can delete their account.
- When an account is deleted, personal data must be removed from the active system.
- Historical exchange records may be anonymized for security, statistics, or system integrity.
- The system must not sell personal data.
- The system must not use personal data for advertising profiling.

---

## Prohibited Activities

The following activities are not allowed:

- Selling goods or services for money
- Using Troc as a payment system
- Using Troc as a cryptocurrency or financial instrument
- Commercial activity by companies or legal entities
- Advertising unrelated products or services
- Offering illegal goods or services
- Offering stolen or counterfeit products
- Harassment, threats, fraud, or abuse
- Creating multiple accounts to manipulate the system

---

## Platform Rules

- Troc is a technology platform for communication and matching.
- Troc does not guarantee the quality of goods or services.
- Troc does not guarantee that an exchange will be completed.
- Troc does not transport, store, sell, buy, or inspect items.
- Troc does not mediate payments because no payments are allowed.
- Troc may suspend accounts that violate the platform rules.

---

## MVP Restrictions

The first version of Troc will not include:

- Payments
- Wallets
- Ratings
- Reviews
- Advertising
- Companies
- Delivery system
- AI recommendations
- Tax calculation
- Paid subscriptions
- Commercial marketplace features

---

## Strategic Principles

- No advertising in MVP.
- No corporate marketplace logic.
- No dependency on money transactions.
- No access for legal entities.
- Direct user-to-user exchange only.
- Local communities first.

- # Reguli de Functionare

## Scop

Acest document defineste regulile fundamentale de functionare ale platformei Troc.

Aceste reguli stabilesc modul in care utilizatorii, produsele, schimburile, mesajele si datele personale functioneaza in cadrul sistemului.

---

## Reguli privind utilizatorii

- Fiecare utilizator trebuie sa fie persoana fizica.
- Persoanele juridice, firmele, ONG-urile, asociatiile, institutiile publice si vanzatorii comerciali nu sunt admisi.
- O adresa de email poate fi folosita pentru un singur cont.
- Utilizatorul trebuie sa accepte Termenii si Conditiile si Politica de Confidentialitate.
- Utilizatorul trebuie sa completeze cel putin judetul si localitatea pentru a putea publica anunturi.
- Utilizatorul isi poate modifica datele de profil.
- Utilizatorul isi poate sterge contul.
- Un cont sters nu poate fi recuperat.

---

## Starea contului

Un cont poate avea una dintre urmatoarele stari:

- activ
- suspendat
- sters

Reguli:

- Utilizatorii activi pot publica produse si pot initia schimburi.
- Utilizatorii suspendati nu pot publica produse si nu pot initia schimburi.
- Utilizatorii stersi nu se mai pot autentifica, iar datele personale trebuie eliminate sau anonimizate.

---

## Reguli privind produsele si serviciile

- Fiecare produs sau serviciu apartine unui singur utilizator.
- Un produs poate reprezenta un bun material sau un serviciu.
- Un produs trebuie sa contina titlu, descriere, categorie, locatie si valoare estimativa in Troc.
- Un produs poate avea una sau mai multe fotografii.
- Produsele ilegale, furate, contrafacute sau interzise nu sunt permise.
- Doar proprietarul poate modifica sau sterge produsul.

---

## Starea produsului

Un produs poate avea una dintre urmatoarele stari:

- disponibil
- rezervat
- schimbat
- retras

Reguli:

- Produsele disponibile pot primi oferte.
- Produsele rezervate sunt blocate temporar pentru un schimb acceptat.
- Produsele schimbate nu mai sunt disponibile.
- Produsele retrase nu mai apar in cautari.

---

## Reguli privind unitatile Troc

- Unitatile Troc reprezinta doar o valoare orientativa.
- Unitatile Troc nu reprezinta bani.
- Unitatile Troc nu pot fi convertite in lei, euro sau alte monede.
- Unitatile Troc nu pot fi cumparate, vandute sau transferate.
- Utilizatorii sunt liberi sa accepte schimburi chiar daca valorile estimate sunt diferite.

---

## Reguli privind schimburile

O oferta de schimb poate avea una dintre urmatoarele stari:

- in asteptare
- acceptata
- respinsa
- anulata
- finalizata

Reguli:

- Un utilizator poate trimite o oferta pentru un produs disponibil.
- Proprietarul poate accepta sau respinge oferta.
- Initiatorul poate anula oferta daca aceasta nu a fost acceptata.
- Dupa acceptare, produsul poate deveni rezervat.
- Dupa finalizare, schimbul nu mai poate fi modificat.
- Platforma Troc nu este parte a schimbului.
- Responsabilitatea finala apartine utilizatorilor implicati.

---

## Reguli privind mesajele

- Mesajele sunt permise doar intre utilizatori interesati de acelasi schimb.
- Sunt interzise amenintarile, hartuirea, spamul, fraudele si activitatile ilegale.
- Utilizatorii sunt responsabili pentru continutul mesajelor trimise.

---

## Protectia datelor personale

- Utilizatorii raman proprietarii datelor personale.
- Utilizatorii pot solicita accesul la datele proprii.
- Utilizatorii pot modifica datele proprii.
- Utilizatorii isi pot sterge contul.
- Datele personale trebuie eliminate din sistemul activ dupa stergerea contului.
- Datele nu vor fi vandute sau utilizate pentru profilare comerciala.
- Platforma respecta cerintele GDPR.

---

## Activitati interzise

Sunt interzise:

- Vanzarea de produse sau servicii contra bani.
- Folosirea platformei ca sistem de plata.
- Folosirea platformei ca moneda sau criptomoneda.
- Activitatile comerciale ale persoanelor juridice.
- Publicitatea nesolicitata.
- Produsele sau serviciile ilegale.
- Produsele furate sau contrafacute.
- Frauda, amenintarile si hartuirea.
- Crearea mai multor conturi pentru manipularea sistemului.

---

## Rolul platformei

- Troc este o platforma tehnologica de conectare intre utilizatori.
- Troc nu garanteaza calitatea produselor sau serviciilor.
- Troc nu garanteaza realizarea unui schimb.
- Troc nu transporta, nu depoziteaza si nu verifica produsele.
- Troc nu proceseaza plati.
- Troc poate suspenda conturile care incalca regulile.

---

## Restrictii MVP

Prima versiune a aplicatiei nu va include:

- Plati
- Portofele digitale
- Rating
- Recenzii
- Publicitate
- Persoane juridice
- Livrare
- Recomandari AI
- Calcul fiscal
- Abonamente platite
- Functionalitati comerciale complexe

---

## Principii Strategice

- Fara publicitate in MVP.
- Fara logica de marketplace comercial.
- Fara tranzactii in bani.
- Fara acces pentru persoane juridice.
- Schimb direct intre utilizatori.
- Comunitatile locale pe primul loc.
