# Legal Framework

## Purpose

Troc is a communication platform that allows private individuals to find each other and voluntarily exchange goods and services.

Troc does not act as a seller, buyer, broker, payment processor, financial institution, or commercial intermediary.

---

## Private Individuals Only

Access to the platform is restricted to private individuals.

Companies, corporations, NGOs, associations, public institutions, and other legal entities are not permitted to use the platform.

---

## No Money Transactions

Troc does not process:

- Cash payments
- Bank transfers
- Credit cards
- Debit cards
- Cryptocurrencies
- Loans
- Financial instruments

All exchanges are voluntary agreements between users.

---

## Troc Units

Troc units are reference values used only to facilitate comparisons between offers.

Troc units:

- Are not money
- Are not a cryptocurrency
- Are not securities
- Are not investment products
- Cannot be exchanged for real currency

---

## User Responsibility

Users are fully responsible for:

- Goods offered
- Services offered
- Information published
- Agreements made with other users

Troc does not guarantee the quality, legality, safety, or availability of any item or service.

---

## Personal Data Protection

Users retain ownership of their personal data.

Users have the right to:

- Access their data
- Correct their data
- Download their data
- Delete their account
- Permanently remove personal information

The platform shall comply with GDPR requirements.

---

## Prohibited Items

The following are prohibited:

- Illegal drugs
- Weapons
- Explosives
- Stolen goods
- Counterfeit products
- Human trafficking
- Illegal services
- Any item prohibited by Romanian or European law

---

## Account Deletion

Users may permanently delete their accounts.

Personal data shall be removed from the active system after account deletion, except where retention is required by law.

---

## Platform Role

Troc is a technology platform.

Troc provides communication and matching services only.

Troc is not a party to exchanges conducted between users.
